We Are Northwest Trucking Company

ADA Express, LLC is a family owned intermodal trucking company, we have trucks running from Seattle/Tacoma ports thru-out Pacific Northwest Region (WA, OR, ID, MT). We also work with owner operators if capacity exceeds our own assets.

Trucking

ADA Express is an experienced and bonded trucking company dedicated to the delivery of full and less-than-container-load shipments, covering Florida and nearby states. To provide premium service, ADA Express has a diverse fleet of line equipment such as chassis, flatbeds, and trailers, as well as special equipment and permits required for handling overweight 20’ and 40’ containers. Because of excellent interchange relationship with all shipping lines, we can pick up your containers at any Southeast port. Working with both company-owned and owner-operator trucks, Southern also contracts a staff of experienced drivers with the knowledge of what it takes to deliver your merchandise safely and on time.
Our drivers are experienced in hauling overweight containers, with the required triaxles (equipment) and permits needed to haul them. Because this network of drivers in location in Florida, Georgia, and South Carolina, this allows us to move manpower from one area to another as demands arise. Additionally, we are now also servicing the Houston Ports. Owning and operating these terminals means we can provide each facility with the support it needs, when it needs it. This ability to utilize company resources fluidly allows us to meet the demands of the business as a whole, ensuring we meet customer needs. We develop and plan out the necessary logistics to move your goods to any U.S. destination and can meet all your full trans-load, trucking, and warehousing needs.

Warehouse

ADA Express warehousing division is a premier with warehousing facilities. We offer 100% supply chain solutions, including local and long haul trucking in and out of facilities. We have warehouse spaces in close proximity to the Ports of Tacoma.

Shipping

ADA Express company carries out container transportation of any level of complexity and guarantees complete safety of the cargo throughout the entire logistics route. Well-established delivery processes and cooperation with reliable carriers around the world allow us to provide quality services on time and at an affordable price.
Container shipping is one of the most economical ways to transport goods. Cargo is transported in removable reusable containers (containers) with a capacity of 20, 40 and 45 feet. Depending on the type of transport used, there are road, air, rail and sea container transportation, which can be carried out both within the state and to international destinations.
