ABOUT ADA EXPRESS

ADA Express - Northwest Trucking Company. We provide our customers with the highest quality service for every mile along the way.

ADA Express, LLC is a family owned intermodal trucking company, we have trucks running from Seattle/Tacoma ports thru-out Pacific Northwest Region (WA, OR, ID, MT). We also work with owner operators if capacity exceeds our own assets. We can handle legal weight containers, 20/40/45 and heavy 40’ containers up to 55,000lbs in product weight, combined gross weight of 92,500 with our 7axle equipment. ADA Express, LLC maintains a strong commitment to a continually improving quality process, allowing us to consistently provide the best service to our customers. We conduct our business with the highest ethical standards. Truth and honesty are essential to the way we operate.
